Chili sin carne

Description

Very fast, easy to make, a bit spicy and refreshing at the same time, one pot mexican dish. This isn't super spicy chili edition, but you can always change it ;)

Ingredients

  • 4 Pieces of garlic
  • 2 Onions
  • 3 Peppers
  • 1 Chilli pepper
  • 300g Tin of red beans
  • 3x 300g Tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 300g Tin of corn
  • Dark chocolate
  • Soy souce
  • Fresh coriander
  • Tomato concentrate
  • Sweet pepper spice
  • Smoked pepper spice
  • Roman cumin
  • Rice
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Start by washing prefered amount of rice, and then cook it in 2 portions of water to 1 portion of rice ratio
  2. Add oil of choice to the pot and then put chopped to little pieces garlic and onions
  3. Add little spoon of sweet pepper, 2 litle spoons of romain cumin and 2 little spoons of smoked papper, and fry it for a while
  4. Then add 3 tins of chopped tomatoes
  5. Next add chopped to little pieces papper and chilli pepper
  6. Add 3 spoons of soy souce
  7. Let it boil for about 10 minutes
  8. Add 1 or 2 squares of chocolate
  9. Wash your beans and corn and then add it to the pot
  10. Chilli is ready, put it into the bowl, add rice on the side, fresh coriander on top, and 2 lime wedges on the side
